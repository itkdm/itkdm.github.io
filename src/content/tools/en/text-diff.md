A practical text comparison tool that supports:

- **Line-level Comparison**: Compare two texts line by line
- **Highlight Display**: Added content highlighted in green, deleted content marked in red
- **Difference Statistics**: Show counts of added, deleted, and unchanged lines
- **Real-time Comparison**: Instant comparison results as you type
- **Clear Display**: Differences at a glance

Perfect for code comparison, document revision, content review, and more.
