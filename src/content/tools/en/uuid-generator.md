A convenient UUID generation tool that supports:

- **UUID v4 Generation**: Generate standard UUID v4 format
- **Batch Generation**: Generate multiple UUIDs at once (up to 100)
- **Format Customization**: Support uppercase/lowercase, with/without hyphens
- **One-click Copy**: Quickly copy single or batch UUIDs
- **Standard Format**: Compliant with RFC 4122 standard

Perfect for development testing, database primary key generation, and more.
