A convenient timestamp conversion tool that supports:

- **Timestamp to Date**: Convert Unix timestamps to readable date/time
- **Date to Timestamp**: Convert date/time to Unix timestamps
- **Multiple Formats**: Support both seconds (10-digit) and milliseconds (13-digit) timestamps
- **Real-time Display**: Show current timestamp and date/time
- **Multiple Output Formats**: ISO format, UTC time, and more

Perfect for development debugging, log analysis, and more.
