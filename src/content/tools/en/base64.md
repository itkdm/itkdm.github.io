A simple and easy-to-use Base64 encoding/decoding tool that supports:

- **Base64 Encoding**: Convert text to Base64 strings
- **Base64 Decoding**: Restore Base64 strings to original text
- **Chinese Support**: Perfect support for Chinese encoding and decoding
- **One-click Copy**: Quickly copy encoded or decoded results
- **Real-time Conversion**: Instant results as you type

Keyboard shortcut: Ctrl/Cmd + Enter to encode/decode quickly
