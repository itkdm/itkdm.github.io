A feature-rich QR code generation tool that supports:

- **Multiple Content Types**: Text, URL, WiFi, Email, Phone, SMS, etc.
- **Customizable Size**: Options from 200x200 to 500x500 pixels
- **Error Correction Levels**: L/M/Q/H four levels for different use cases
- **Preset Templates**: Built-in common format templates for quick generation
- **One-click Download**: Generated QR codes can be downloaded as PNG images

Perfect for sharing links, WiFi passwords, contact information, and more.
