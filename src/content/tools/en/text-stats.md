A comprehensive text statistics tool that supports:

- **Basic Statistics**: Character count (with/without spaces), word count, line count, paragraph count
- **Character Classification**: Count Chinese characters, English characters, numbers
- **Word Frequency Analysis**: Analyze word frequency and display Top 20 high-frequency words
- **Real-time Statistics**: Instant update as you type
- **Chinese & English Support**: Perfect support for mixed Chinese and English text

Perfect for writing assistance, content analysis, SEO optimization, and more.
