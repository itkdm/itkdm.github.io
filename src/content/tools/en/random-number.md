A powerful random number generation tool that supports:

- **Custom Range**: Set minimum and maximum values
- **Integer/Float**: Choose to generate integers or floating-point numbers
- **Batch Generation**: Generate multiple random numbers at once (up to 1000)
- **Duplicate Option**: Choose whether to allow duplicate numbers
- **Statistics**: Display count, minimum, maximum, average
- **One-click Copy**: Quickly copy individual random numbers

Perfect for test data generation, lottery, random selection, and more.
